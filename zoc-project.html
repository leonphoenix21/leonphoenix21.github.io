Zocamhost Web Hosting Platform

Zocamhost is a web hosting platform offering services like WordPress, dedicated, VPS, and cloud hosting. Before this project, it primarily supported PHP-based software, including WordPress and no-code systems. This project focused on expanding to VPS, shared, and reseller hosting, improving server performance, attracting higher-margin clients, and enabling scalable hosting for internal applications.

Challenges & Solutions

Problem: Initially relied on platforms like A2 Hosting and AWS, which became costly and less scalable as the client base grew.

Solution: Transitioned to more cost-effective providers and established data centers in Atlanta, Argentina, and France for global redundancy and improved uptime.

Problem: The zocamhost.com domain was controlled by a competitor, leading to a revocation when our VPS service grew.

Solution: Quickly switched to zocamhost.cloud and rerouted services with zero downtime. Becoming a domain registrar prevented future domain ownership issues.

Problem: An incorrectly executed script caused accidental deletion of some client data.

Solution: Collaborated with datacenter providers to retrieve backups and restore all affected accounts. Introduced an automatic backup system for scripts affecting multiple client sites and daily backups for all clients.

Problem: One of our data centers unexpectedly went offline post-launch, causing client downtime.

Solution: Quickly rerouted hosting services to alternative data centers to minimize disruptions, reinforcing the need for robust failover systems.

Problem: Balancing backend operations and client support became increasingly difficult with limited staff.

Solution: Implemented a collaborative support system: Its Everyones Responsibility That Somebody Does It. All team members were notified of new tickets, ensuring faster response times and efficient issue resolution.
